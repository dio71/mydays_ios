1. Summary

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, location, advertising identifiers, etc.). The App does not require an account or sign-in.

iCloud Sync (CloudKit)

The App can use Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your devices. This data is stored in your own iCloud account and is subject to Apple's iCloud Terms and Privacy Policy. We do not view or collect this data.

Health Data (HealthKit)

If you set an activity goal and grant permission, the App reads your step count, distance, active energy, and flights climbed on a read-only basis to display goal progress. This data is used on device only and is never transmitted externally or stored on our servers. Health data is never used for any marketing purpose.

Speech Recognition

When you use voice input, your speech is converted to text using on-device speech recognition and used as an item title. Voice data is not stored or transmitted externally.

4. Third Parties and Data Sharing

We do not share your data with, or delegate its processing to, any third party. The App contains no analytics or advertising SDKs.

We rely on Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) as storage infrastructure, but that data remains within your own account and is inaccessible to us.

5. Your Rights (GDPR and Others)

Because we do not collect or retain your personal data, all data remains entirely under your control.

  • Right to erasure: Use the "Delete All Data" feature in the App to remove all data from your device and iCloud at any time. Deleting the App removes on-device data; iCloud data can be managed in your device's iCloud settings.
  • Right of access and portability: All data resides on your device and is directly accessible to you.
  • Residents of the EU, EEA, and UK have rights under the GDPR; residents of the Republic of Korea have rights under the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA). Since we hold no personal data, you can exercise these rights directly without submitting a request to us.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal data and is safe for users of all ages (4+).

7. Security

Data is stored on your device and within Apple's iCloud infrastructure (subject to Apple's security policies). Because no data is transmitted to our servers, there is no risk of a breach on our side.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in law or app functionality. For material changes, we will notify you via an in-app notice or by updating the effective date on this page.
