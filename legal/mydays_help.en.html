GETTING STARTED

What's the difference between tasks and goals?

A task is something you do once or within a period (e.g., a meeting, a deadline). A goal is an ongoing habit or abstention. Goals are tracked with progress and streaks.

What are the 4 goal types (Abstain · Activity · Focus · Habit)?

  • Abstain — Refrain from something for a set time (e.g., fasting). Tracked with a countdown.
  • Activity — A numeric target (e.g., 10,000 steps, 2L water). Steps/distance can sync from the Health app.
  • Focus — Stay focused for a set duration (e.g., study 2h), via the focus screen.
  • Habit — A recurring habit you check off (e.g., reading, vitamins).

How do I set up a recurring item?

In the item editor, turn on Repeat and choose daily / weekly (weekdays) / monthly (dates or rules) / yearly. To stop a recurrence, set a repeat end date.

WIDGETS & AUTO-TRACKING

How do I add a widget?

  • Home Screen: long-press empty space → + (top-left) → search "MyDays" → choose Tasks / Goals / Tasks & Goals.
  • Lock Screen: long-press the lock screen → "Customize" → tap a widget area → add MyDays.
[Screenshot: adding a widget / widget types]

My activity (e.g., steps) isn't updating automatically.

Activity goals (steps, distance, calories, stairs) need Health app permission. Allow it in Settings > Info & Permissions > Health permissions, or in Health > Sharing > Apps > MyDays. Open the app once after granting to refresh.

I'm not getting notifications.

Check that Notifications are enabled in Settings > Info & Permissions > App permissions. Per-item reminders can be turned on in the item editor.

SYNC · DATA

How do I use it across multiple devices?

Sign in with the same iCloud account and your data syncs automatically (see the last sync time in Settings > Sync). No separate account or sign-up.

What does the Pro Plan add?

All core features are free. The Pro Plan adds unlimited usage (goals, recurrences, categories, checklists), theme colors, and activity reports. One-time purchase, yours forever.

How do I back up or delete my data?

In Settings > Info & Permissions > Data, you can Export Data (JSON backup) and Delete All Data.
